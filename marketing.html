Overview

We are seeking a motivated Marketing Intern to join our team. As a Sales and Marketing Intern, you will play a vital role in supporting our sales and marketing efforts. This is an excellent opportunity to gain practical experience in a fast-paced business environment and develop essential skills in sales and marketing.

Responsibilities

  • Assist in creating marketing campaigns and strategies to promote our products/services.
  • Conduct market research to identify potential customers and competitors.
  • Help manage and update our company's social media accounts.
  • Assist in organizing and attending promotional events or trade shows.
  • Collaborate with the sales team to identify and pursue new business opportunities.
  • Support the sales team in preparing sales presentations and materials.
  • Assist in generating leads and following up on potential customers.
  • Help maintain the customer relationship management (CRM) database.
  • Participate in sales meetings and training sessions.
  • Contribute to writing and editing marketing materials, such as blog posts, newsletters, and advertisements.
  • Perform administrative tasks related to sales and marketing activities.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in sales and marketing.
  • Assist in analyzing marketing data and preparing reports.

This job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Duties, responsibilities, and activities may change at any time with or without notice.

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a high school program or equivalent: Actively pursuing a high school diploma or equivalent educational qualification.

Job-related experience

  • Customarily has at least one or more years of collections experience

Job-Type

  • Remote

Job-related skills/competencies
  • Ability to make decisions or solve problems by using logic to identify key facts, explore alternatives, and propose quality solutions
  • Strong organizational skills and ability to manage multiple tasks
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Demonstrated initiative, problem-solving abilities, and resourcefulness
  • Adaptability and flexibility in a fast-paced environment
  • High level of professionalism, integrity, and discretion
